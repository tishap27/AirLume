Core Capabilities

Moving beyond reactive lightning detection to predictive intelligence for aviation operations.

Predictive Formation Analysis

Advanced physics models analyze real-time atmospheric conditions to predict lightning formation 60-90 minutes before strikes occur along specific flight routes.

Precision Route Optimization

Targeted route adjustments avoid actual lightning risks while minimizing detours to 10-30 miles instead of 100+ mile storm system avoidance.

Multi-Altitude Assessment

Comprehensive lightning probability analysis at different flight levels (FL200-FL400) with optimal cruise altitude recommendations to reduce exposure.

Problem Statement

Airlines face a costly dilemma with current lightning detection systems.

Costly Inspections

Approximately $2 billion spent annually on mandatory post-strike inspections across the industry, even when aircraft protection systems function perfectly.

Inefficient Routing

Current systems require avoiding entire storm systems by 100+ miles, causing massive delays and fuel waste as air traffic increases.

Reactive Systems

Existing lightning detection only identifies strikes after they occur, providing no advance route optimization capability.
