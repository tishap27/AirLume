Add: Improved physics model and SWDI validation

Major improvements:
- Remove broken CAPE calculation (was giving 10-20x too low values)
- Fix E-field risk thresholds: lower bounds from 1000 V/m to 400 V/m
- Improve threshold granularity: 400/700/1000/1500/2500 V/m tiers
- Redistribute risk weights: E-field 45%, ice 25%, humidity 15%, pressure 10%

Validation methods enhanced:
- Integrate SWDI (Space Weather Data Inventory) for real lightning strike data
- Download 181 lightning events from 2024 (69,493 total storm events)
- Validate against entire 2024 dataset (60-day filter currently no results yet )
- Test 50 randomly sampled strikes

Results:
- Detection rate improved from 46.3% → 53.2% (CSV validation) → 68.0% (SWDI validation)
- Average E-field: 533.5 V/m (max: 930.0 V/m)
- Average risk: 30.8% (max: 55.0%)
- 34/50 strikes correctly predicted as HIGH risk (≥25% threshold)
